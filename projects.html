Film Management

This film management software allows you to view, add, edit and delete films.

There is also a button at the bottom left that allows you to switch between dark and light mode.

All films that have been added are stored in an SQL database.

Blue Dragon

At the beginning of the game, you can choose a weapon.

After that, new enemies appear repeatedly. After defeating ten enemies, you advance to the next world.

When you defeat an enemy, you have a 20% chance of receiving a healing potion.

The game ends when you die or have survived all worlds.

Tamagotchi

In this game, you have your own Tamagotchi that you have to take care of.

You have to feed it, let it play and put it to sleep. To feed it, you have to keep fishing for fish.

If you don't take care of your Tamagotchi, it will become sad and may even die.

Snake

In my Snake game, you can play Snake. You can control the white snake using the WASD keys.

As soon as the snake eats a red fruit, your high score increases by one and the snake becomes one pixel longer.

As soon as you hit the wall, the game is over.
